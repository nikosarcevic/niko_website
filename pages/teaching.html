Teaching

I am passionate about sharing knowledge and mentoring the next generation of cosmologists and astrophysicists. Below are some highlights of my teaching and mentoring activities:

  • Cosmology Courses: Designed and taught advanced courses on cosmology, focusing on weak lensing, galaxy formation, and large-scale structure.
  • Physics Labs: Supervised undergraduate and graduate laboratory sessions, fostering hands-on learning in experimental physics.
  • Student Mentorship: Guided students in research projects related to cosmology, data analysis, and model development.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Delivered specialized workshops and seminars on cosmological inference and observational techniques.

Through teaching, I aim to inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and a deep understanding of the universe in my students.
