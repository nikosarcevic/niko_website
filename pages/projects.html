Projects

Here are some of the exciting projects I am part of:

NASA Roman Cosmology Project

As a member of the Roman Cosmology Project Infrastructure Team (PIT), I work on cosmological inference and weak lensing analysis to enhance our understanding of dark energy and the universe's large-scale structure.

LSST DESC

I am actively involved in the Legacy Survey of Space and Time Dark Energy Science Collaboration, focusing on weak lensing systematics, cosmological modeling, and data analysis techniques to prepare for LSST data.

Intrinsic Alignments

My research in intrinsic alignments addresses their impact on weak lensing signals. I develop models and mitigation techniques to improve the accuracy of cosmological measurements.

XENON Collaboration

In previous work, I contributed to the XENON collaboration, where I designed the electric field cage of the XENONnT detector, advancing the search for dark matter particles.
