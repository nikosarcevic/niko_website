Projects

Explore the major projects I have been involved in, which focus on advancing our understanding of cosmology and astrophysics:

  • LSST DESC: Working on weak lensing, galaxy bias models, and intrinsic alignments to help prepare for the era of large-scale sky surveys.
  • Roman Space Telescope: Contributing to understanding strong and weak lensing signals to explore dark energy and galaxy formation.
  • CosmoCast: Developing a cosmological forecasting pipeline for data analysis and model predictions.
  • Intrinsic Alignments: Investigating the impact of intrinsic alignments on cosmological inferences in observational surveys.

These projects aim to bridge theoretical models with observational data, pushing the boundaries of what we know about the cosmos.
